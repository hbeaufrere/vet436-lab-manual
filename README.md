# VET 436 — Small Mammal Medicine

## Laboratory Manual

**UC Davis School of Veterinary Medicine — 2025**

---

*Hugues Beaufrere, Michelle Hawkins, David Guzman, Krista Keller, Ariella Darvish, Marina Liles*

---

This laboratory manual accompanies the VET 436 Small Mammal Medicine course at UC Davis. It contains handouts for three hands-on laboratory sessions covering the clinical anatomy, physical examination techniques, and dentistry of rabbits and rodents.

### Labs Included

| Lab | Topic | Focus |
|-----|-------|-------|
| **Lab 1** | Clinical Anatomy | Prosections of ferret, guinea pig, rat, and rabbit with dissection |
| **Lab 2** | Physical Examination & Clinical Techniques | Rabbit handling, full physical exam, venipuncture, nasolacrimal flush |
| **Lab 3** | Dentistry of Rabbits & Rodents | Oral anatomy, occlusal adjustments, incisor extraction |

> **Note:** These materials are designed to complement the lecture content delivered in VET 436. Students should review pre-lab videos and lecture notes prior to each session.
