# Learning Outcomes

1. **Perform a thorough physical examination of a rabbit** including full oral and aural examinations.
2. **Identify two different intramuscular injection sites** for use in the rabbit.
3. **Identify no less than 3 potential venipuncture sites** for use in the rabbit.
4. **Identify sites for subcutaneous injection** for use in the rabbit.
5. **Perform venipuncture** from the lateral saphenous vein.
6. **Perform nasolacrimal duct flush** and determine duct patency.
7. **Perform safe restraint and handling** of rabbits.
