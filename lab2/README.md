# Lab 2: Physical Examination and Clinical Techniques

**VET-436 / 2025**

*Hugues Beaufrere, Michelle Hawkins, Krista Keller, David Guzman, Ariella Darvish, Marina Liles*

---

In this lab, you will practice **handling** rabbits, performing a complete rabbit **physical examination**, and practicing a few important clinical techniques including **venipuncture, subcutaneous injections, and nasolacrimal duct flushing**.

Please review the videos in the lab module prior to the lab.

> **The learning outcomes are:**
> 1. Perform a thorough physical examination of a rabbit including full oral and aural examinations.
> 2. Identify two different intramuscular injection sites for use in the rabbit.
> 3. Identify no less than 3 potential venipuncture sites for use in the rabbit.
> 4. Identify sites for subcutaneous injection for use in the rabbit.
> 5. Perform venipuncture from the lateral saphenous vein.
> 6. Perform nasolacrimal duct flush and determine duct patency.
> 7. Perform safe restraint and handling of rabbits.

Rabbits have been sedated with midazolam.
