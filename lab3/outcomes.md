# Learning Outcomes

1. **Evaluate the dental and maxillofacial anatomy** of rabbits and rodents.
2. **Compare and contrast the dental anatomy** of rabbits and rodents and infer clinical applications of observed differences between species.
3. **Perform occlusal adjustments** of incisors and cheek teeth in rabbits and rodents applying adequate dentistry techniques.
4. **Perform extraction of incisors** in rabbits.
