# Lab 3: Dentistry of Rabbits and Rodents

**VET-436**

*Hugues Beaufrere, David Guzman, Michelle Hawkins, Krista Keller, and Residents*

---

> **The learning objectives of this lab are:**
> - Evaluate the dental and maxillofacial anatomy of rabbits and rodents.
> - Compare and contrast the dental anatomy of rabbits and rodents and infer clinical applications of observed differences between species.
> - Perform occlusal adjustments of incisors and cheek teeth in rabbits and rodents applying adequate dentistry techniques.
> - Perform extraction of incisors in rabbits.

Please **bring your labcoat**. You will need to also wear other PPE such as goggles, mask, cap, and gloves.

Some anatomical illustrations are provided in the appendix. They are from an amazing book entitled *A Colour Atlas of Anatomy of Small Laboratory Animals*. Popesko P et al, Saunders, 1992.
